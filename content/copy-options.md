# Hero headline options

Register: Metalab. Serif, lowercase, under 7 words, one italic word permitted.
The live build currently uses **Option 1**. Swap in `content/home.ts` → `hero.headlineLines`.

## Option 1 — automotive, run like *infrastructure*.

Operator-first. Positions the group as the serious, systemic layer under
everyday driving. Reads institutional without a single buzzword.

## Option 2 — we operate what *moves* you.

Warmer. Puts the group behind the driver's daily life, still operator-voiced
("we operate", not "we imagine"). Works well over the interchange plate.

## Option 3 — four companies. one *standard*.

The bluntest of the three. States the group thesis in five words and lets the
company chips do the explaining. Strongest for skeptical corp-dev visitors.
